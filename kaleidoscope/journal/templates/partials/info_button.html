Need a hand getting started?

Here’s how you can make the most out of your journaling experience:

  • Home Sweet Home: Click the logo at the top left or the home button in the navbar to return to the homepage at any time.
  • Manage Your Account: Customize your account settings from the top right corner.
  • Create a New Journal Entry: Begin a fresh entry by clicking the "Create Entry" button on the left sidebar.
  • Save Your Thoughts: Don’t lose a word. Use the save button on the navbar to keep your entries safe.
  • Share Your Journey: Send a PDF version of your journal entry via email with the button on the navbar.
  • Download Your Day: Hit the download button on the navbar to save your journal entry to your PC.
  • Visualize Your Story: Enhance your entries by adding images with the "Add Image" button on the navbar. Click images to resize or hover and click x to delete an image!
  • Templates at Your Fingertips: Use the sidebar on the right to craft your entry from an existing template.
  • Inspiration Station: Lacking inspiration? Type what you did today in the AI-connected search box, and it will suggest creative prompts.
  • Mood Tracker: Set the tone of your journal entry by adjusting the mood slider to reflect how you feel.

All your writing and editing magic happens in the central part of the screen. So go ahead, make today's entry memorable!
